Find Coordinates

Enter one of the following:

  • an address
  • a city and state/province
  • latitude and longitude, separated by a comma (example: 45.0735,-73.2087)

and then drag the marker to tweak the location. The latitude/longitude coordinates will appear in the infowindow after each drag or button click.
